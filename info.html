Keep being creative!

So many people have told me this after speaking to me.

Yes, it is a process full of challenges and opportunities. It is so exciting because we are so new and fresh. We have so many possibilities though it is so easy to doubt a lot about ourselves.

“Do not try to work in the suite, you just cannot do it”, Elliott said. I think it is because we were born with creativity and we live for creativity.

If you are the same kind of person, keep being creative and do not give up.
